Partnerships

Art Tap has been granted two consecutive Parsons Entrepreneurial Lab (Elab) fellowships. We work closely with Parsons faculty and mentors to foster the growth of our platform and our art-loving community. If you are interested in being a sponsor at an Art Tap event, partnering with us to promote a cause, or collaborating on an exciting project, we’d love to hear from you.

We are open to partnerships with:

  • Art fairs and shows
  • Galleries
  • Art schools and ateliers
  • Local and national arts organizations
  • Museums
  • Art magazines, blogs, and content providers
  • Art supply stores
  • Interior designers, decorators, and other trade professionals
  • Charities promoting visual art or preserving artistic heritage
  • Financial institutions
