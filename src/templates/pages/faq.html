FAQs

What is Art Tap?

Art is for everyone, right? We think so. So we launched Art Tap to unlock gallery doors and break down studio walls (metaphorically, that is) by putting art in the hands of enthusiasts everywhere.

We’re a curated marketplace for emerging and seasoned professional fine artists. Each artist selected for the site has the ability to build their own profile and manage the sales of their original artwork. We sell original paintings, photographs, works on paper, limited edition prints, and mixed media artwork. All art is priced between $100-$10,000 USD and Art Tap receieves only half the commission of traditional art galleries. All these aspects make it more affordable for art collectors, interior decorators, investors and even first time art buyers, to own profesional art.

We believe that by making art more accessible and enjoyable, we can empower artists, create meaningful relationships between people with a shared interests in art and broaden our horizons by experiencing beautiful, thought-provoking, and expressive artwork.
