How It Works

Step 1

Fill out the free application and our curators will review your work. There are no monthly or annual fees to maintain an Art Tap account. We only receive a commission when your work is sold.

Step 2

Once approved, build a personalized artist page. Fill out a profile, upload your art, and post news and events. We allow artists the flexibility to price, sell, and ship artworks on their terms.

Step 3

Sell pieces easily and securely through Art Tap to a large network of art buyers. Access our online resources and exclusive events. Best of all, spend more time creating and less time marketing.

Artist Benefits

Quality

Our dedicated team of curators carfully select emerging and prominent artists to exhibit on Art Tap, always ensuring your work is placed amongst other professional works.

Flexibility

We offer artists the flexibility to price, sell, and ship artworks on their terms. Artists do not need to sign an exclusivity contract to sell through Art Tap.

More Profit

Traditional galleries receive over 50% commission on the sale of each piece. Alternatively, Art Tap receieves less than 30% commission while fulfilling gallery responsibilities at no added cost.

Promotion

Our team will promote your upcoming events and feature you and your work for free through our various marketing channels.

Community

By registering, artists join a supportive worldwide community. There are always opportunities to meet prospective buyers and learn from other artists and art enthusiasts.

Trust

All transactions and payment information is 100% secure.
